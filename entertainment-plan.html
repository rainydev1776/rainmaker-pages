Rainmaker Entertainment — Release Plan

Distro-first: Rainmaker lays the rail; music and Spawn ride it.

Last updated · 2026-04-21

Core thesis

Rainmaker is the distribution rail, not just a product. Releasing Spawn or catalog music in isolation asks cold algorithms to do what this stack was built to do: proof, habit, community, and repeat touchpoints.

Sequence: prove the rail in public → compound creator relationships → run parallel music awareness (wildposting, CMG/Torsten, clippers) → seed tracks in-stream only after the room trusts outcomes → then name Rainmaker Entertainment as the umbrella.

Phases

P0Rail definition

Define what “distribution” means in your world.

  • Signup path + first-win moment + retention loop are explicit.
  • One hero metric you are willing to show publicly (internal KPIs can stay richer).
P1Rainmaker in the wild

Proof before poetry.

  • Auger + clippers: attach to outcomes viewers can feel (wins, streaks, payouts, clips)—not the label yet.
  • Viewers graduate from “what app?” to “how do I get his link?”
P2Parallel music channels

Top of funnel without stealing the spine.

  • Wildposting for music culture and recall.
  • Torsten → CMG: clear asks (playlist, sync, distro path)—start small if needed.
  • Clipper network: repeatable brief + turnaround SLA.
P3Relationship compounding on stream

Earn the right to seed music.

  • After repeated exposure + social proof + some fans actually making money.
  • Background music becomes environmental branding—the room already believes in Rainmaker.
P4Rainmaker Entertainment (named)

One clean public moment.

  • Name, visual system, roster or first releases, and why the umbrella exists.
  • Spawn and catalog ship as cargo on the channel you opened, not a second channel from zero.
P5Sustain

Same rail, more cargo.

  • Auger keeps the catalog in rotation organically.
  • Wildposting + CMG scale off-platform; clippers turn live moments into discovery.

Phase gates

Do not advance until the prior gate reads true.

Failure modes

  • ✕ Parallel launches: Spawn + isolated music + big Rainmaker push in the same cycle splits attention and attribution.
  • ✕ Music before proof: tracks read as generic content instead of score for a world people already believe in.
  • ✕ Over-mystifying without compliance hygiene: creative “no name” moments still need correct disclosures where rules apply.

On-stream music

If placement is paid, sponsored, or materially connected to a commercial release, follow platform and jurisdictional disclosure rules so the rollout stays durable.
